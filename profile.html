Criminal Defense & Bail Expertise

Facing a criminal charge can be life-altering. The police investigation is often biased, and without immediate legal intervention, you risk jail time. We specialize in:

  • Bail Matters (Section 437/439 CrPC): Expert in securing Regular Bail from Sessions Court and High Court when lower courts reject it.
  • Anticipatory Bail (Section 438 CrPC): Protecting clients from arrest in false cases like 498A (Dowry) or SC/ST Act.
  • Heinous Crimes Defense: Specialized defense strategies for Murder (302 IPC), Attempt to Murder (307 IPC), and Rape (376 IPC) cases.
  • NDPS Act (Drug Cases): Challenging procedural lapses in recovery and seizure to secure bail in commercial quantity cases.
  • FIR Quashing (Section 482 CrPC): approaching the High Court to quash false FIRs that are filed due to personal vendetta.
